About Us

The foundation of the Shri Sai Dham temple was laid around 45 years ago.However the registration and development of the temple was recently done by Murli Prasad Choudhary. Situated at Borivali east ,the temple is dedicated to divine Shri Sai Baba which fulfills the desires of worshippers.

The temple has idols of Shri Sai Baba at the center along with idols of lord Shri Ram and Shri Hanuman on either sides. On entering the temple,the worshippers gets darshan of three dieties i.e Shri Sai Baba, Shri Ganesh, Durga Mata, Shivling and Lord Hanuman. The throne of the deities is beautifully carved and elegantly decorated.

Management Committee
