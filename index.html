Overview of Temple

The foundation of the Shri Sai Dham temple was laid around 45 years ago.However the registration and development of the temple was recently done by Murli Prasad Choudhary.
Situated at Borivali east ,the temple is dedicated to divine Shri Sai Baba which fulfills the desires of worshippers.

The trust enthusiastically involves itself in welfare and development of society.The trust carries out activities which includes food distribution(Anna Dan),health care camps for the poor,organizing various cultural and religious events.
